Research

Precision rheumatology shaped by multi-omics.

The work focuses on disease heterogeneity, immune profiling, tissue biology, and computational stratification.

Themes

Core research areas.

Research Approach

From patient to mechanism to precision care.

1

Patient Cohorts

Clinical phenotyping, biobanking, and deep characterisation of myositis, SLE, and related autoimmune diseases in specialist rheumatology centres.

2

Multi-Omics Profiling

Paired peripheral blood immunophenotyping, bulk and single-cell transcriptomics, and integrative analysis across disease states and tissue compartments.

3

Computational Analysis

Machine learning classification, dimensionality reduction, pathway enrichment, and cross-cohort validation to identify stable disease subgroups and biomarker signatures.

4

Clinical Translation

Linking molecular stratification to treatment response, prognostic prediction, and consensus frameworks for precision rheumatology practice worldwide.
