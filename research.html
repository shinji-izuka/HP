Research Design

A translational workflow instead of isolated methods.

病態観察からサンプル、オミクス、計算、層別化、臨床実装までを一本の流れとして設計します。

1

Clinical signal

Start from heterogeneity that matters in real rheumatology care: flare pattern, organ involvement, response, prognosis.

2

Sample and phenotype

Integrate patient cohorts, biospecimens, immunophenotyping, and detailed phenotype annotation.

3

Transcriptome and omics

Use blood and tissue transcriptome layers to capture immune programs and disease-state transitions.

4

Computational analysis

Apply machine learning, clustering, and integrative bioinformatics to expose clinically meaningful structure.

5

Stratification

Define subgroups that can inform diagnosis, monitoring, risk estimation, and therapeutic choice.

6

Translation

Bring findings back to disease mechanism, target discovery, and more precise clinical frameworks.
