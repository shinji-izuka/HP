Narrative

A physician-scientist trajectory grounded in rheumatology practice.

Public University of Tokyo department material describes a path beginning with graduation from Tottori University in 2016, followed by early clinical training and rheumatology training at the National Center for Global Health and Medicine.

In 2021, that path shifts toward doctoral work at The University of Tokyo, where clinical questions are expanded into immune phenotyping, transcriptomics, and computational analysis. The JSPS DC1 fellowship from April 2022 to March 2025 and the current public listing as a Specially Appointed Clinical Fellow show how the clinical and research tracks have remained tightly connected.
