About Me

  • Specialty: Rheumatology
  • Degree: M.D., Ph.D. in Medicine

I investigate the pathogenesis of rheumatic diseases such as myositis and systemic lupus erythematosus, integrating clinical insights with multi‑omics bioinformatics to enable precision medicine.
