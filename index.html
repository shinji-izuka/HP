About Me

Affiliation: Department of Allergy and Rheumatology, The University of Tokyo Hospital

Specialty: Rheumatologist

Degree: M.D., Ph.D. in Medicine

I am dedicated to understanding the pathogenesis and developing novel treatments for rheumatic diseases, particularly autoimmune conditions like myositis and Systemic Lupus Erythematosus (SLE). My research integrates clinical information with bioinformatics techniques to stratify patients and advance personalized medicine. I recently completed my Ph.D., focusing on these innovative approaches.
